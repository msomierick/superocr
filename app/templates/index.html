Upload an image to get its OCR and Exif information as JSON

Choose an image and hit the upload button

This endpoint allows users to an upload image and get its OCR and Exif metadata as a JSON response.

For the OCR data, the project uses Google's Tesseract-OCR Engine to recognise and read the text embedded in images, a form of optical character recognition (OCR).

After uploading just click back button to upload another image.
